Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Gagana (ගගන) – Astronomy App ("we", "our", or "the App") collects, shares, and handles your information to provide a better celestial experience.

1. Data Safety & Overview

We are committed to being transparent about how your data is handled. Below is a breakdown of the data our app collects and shares, adhering to Google Play's Data Safety guidelines.

Data Collected

This app may collect the following data types to function and improve performance:

  • Location: Approximate location (used to provide accurate star maps based on your position).
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and Diagnostics.
  • Device or other IDs: Device IDs (including Android Advertising ID).

Data Shared

Data that may be shared with other companies or organizations (such as ad networks and analytics providers):

  • Location: Approximate location.
  • App Info and Performance: Crash logs and Diagnostics.
  • App Activity: App interactions.
  • Device or other IDs: Device IDs.

2. Security Practices

Your data security is important to us. Data is encrypted in transit. This means your data is transferred over a secure connection to protect it from unauthorized access.

3. Data Deletion Policy

Currently, the developer has not provided a way to request data deletion. Because we do not require user accounts or store personal identification information directly on our own servers, there is no centralized profile to delete. Data collected by third-party services (like AdMob) is managed by those services.

4. Advertising and Third-Party Services

This App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may use your Device ID (Advertising ID) and approximate location to serve personalized ads and for analytics. For more information, visit How Google uses information from sites or apps that use our services.

5. GDPR and User Consent

For users in the European Economic Area (EEA) and the UK, we comply with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). We obtain your explicit consent before serving personalized advertisements. You can manage your consent preferences within the App settings.

6. Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
